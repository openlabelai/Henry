### AI Parser Bug Fix — MERGED to main ✅ (commit `c2f47f7`)
- Seb uploaded "2025 - Y1 BLANKO Sales Export.csv" — got "AI confidence too low"
- Root cause: `revenueVerifier.ts` scored checksum=50 (not found) with 35% weight
  - Pulled total revenue confidence to ~55
  - Veto rule: `confidence < 70` → hard cap at 49 → self-validation rejected
  - ANY custom export without a totals row would fail this way
- Fix: when `checksum.found = false`, exclude checksum from weighted average entirely
  - Renormalise remaining signals (fingerprint 0.20, sampleVerification 0.20, crossUpload 0.10, distribution 0.15) to sum to 1.0
  - Checksum veto rules (90 floor / 30 cap) preserved — only fire when checksum IS found
- **Lesson: Revenue verifier veto was miscalibrated for non-standard exports. Absence of a summary row ≠ wrong mapping.**
